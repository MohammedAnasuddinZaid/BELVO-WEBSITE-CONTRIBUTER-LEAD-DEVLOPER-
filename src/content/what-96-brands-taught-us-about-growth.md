We've worked with 96+ brands. Skincare startups, fashion labels, restaurants, EdTech companies, fintech platforms — across 13 different industries.

Here's what we've learned.

## 1. Speed Beats Perfection

The brands that grew fastest were not the ones with the most polished content. They were the ones that **shipped fast, tested fast, and iterated fast**. Done is better than perfect — especially in social media.

## 2. Niche Down to Scale Up

The brands that tried to speak to everyone ended up speaking to no one. The ones that niched down hard — "skincare for men in their 30s" instead of "skincare for everyone" — scaled faster and built stronger communities.

## 3. Data Without Creativity Is Dead

Analytics tell you what happened. Creativity tells you what to do next. The best campaigns we've run combined both — a strong creative hook backed by data-driven targeting.

## 4. The First 90 Days Are Everything

Brand perception is set early. If the first 90 days of a brand's social presence are inconsistent, poorly positioned, or off-brand — it takes 3–4x longer to correct. Get it right from day one.

## 5. Relationships > Reach

Every high-performing brand in our portfolio has a strong community relationship. Responding to DMs, engaging with comments, reposting UGC — these small actions compound into massive trust over time.

## The BELVO Approach

We don't just run campaigns. We build brands. Every strategy we create is rooted in these learnings — tested across real brands, real industries, real results.
