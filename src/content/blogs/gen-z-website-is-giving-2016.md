# Your Website Is Giving 2016 (And Not in a Cute Way)

Your business has evolved. Your website still looks like it remembers when "mobile-friendly" was an optional upgrade. That mismatch is quietly costing you attention, trust, and enquiries.

## Your Hero Section Is Saying Everything Except the Point

Visitors should understand what you do, who it is for, and what they should do next within seconds. If the first screen is a paragraph, four buttons, three animations, and a motivational quote, nobody knows where to look.

Keep the promise clear. Use one strong headline, one supporting sentence, and one primary action. Clarity is not boring. Confusion is.

## Mobile Is Not the Shrunk Version

A responsive website should adapt to the user's screen and way of interacting. That means readable text, comfortable spacing, touch-friendly buttons, images that stay inside the viewport, and no surprise horizontal scrolling.

If your mobile layout feels like the desktop site was squeezed into a lunchbox, it needs a rethink.

## Slow Is a Design Problem Too

A gorgeous page that takes forever to become useful is still a bad experience. Loading speed, interaction response, and layout stability are part of how professional your brand feels.

Compress heavy media, remove scripts that add no value, reserve space for images, and test the experience on an average phone - not only on office Wi-Fi.

## Every Page Needs a Next Move

People should never reach the end of a page and wonder, "Cool... now what?" Guide them toward one relevant next step: explore a service, see the work, request a proposal, or book a call.

BELVO combines web strategy, design, development, and conversion thinking to create websites that look current and make the next action obvious.

Your website does not need more decoration. It needs fewer reasons to leave.
